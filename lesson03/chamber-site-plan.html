Site Name

The site name is "Nairobi Chamber of Commerce". This name represents a city in which the chamber operates.

Purpose

The Nairobi Chamber of Commerce provides a platform for local businesses to showcase their products and services, enhancing their visibility and attracting potential customers. It fosters business networking, offering valuable resources, events, and information, helping members connect and grow their enterprises.
